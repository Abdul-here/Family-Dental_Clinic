Comprehensive Dental Care

Every treatment you need, delivered by specialists you can trust.

Basal Implants

Advanced implant technique by Dr. Younas Baloch — a trusted specialist. Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement with faster healing and high success rates. Ideal for patients who need immediate loading or have insufficient bone density for traditional implants.

Book Appointment

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free procedure to save infected or damaged teeth. Our modern techniques and experienced team ensure patient comfort throughout the entire process. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the treatment feels.

Book Appointment

Tooth Extraction

Safe and gentle removal of damaged, decayed, or impacted teeth. We use refined techniques to minimise discomfort and provide clear aftercare guidance to ensure fast, smooth recovery.

Book Appointment

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening treatments that remove deep stains and deliver a visibly brighter, more confident smile. Results are immediate and dramatically more effective than over-the-counter options.

Book Appointment

Scaling & Polishing

Deep professional cleaning that removes tartar, plaque, and surface stains. Recommended every 6 months to maintain healthy gums and prevent more serious dental issues from developing.

Book Appointment

Braces & Orthodontics

Corrective treatment for misaligned teeth and bite issues. We design personalised orthodontic plans for children, teens, and adults — including traditional braces and modern clear aligner options.

Book Appointment

Fillings & Restorations

Natural-looking, tooth-coloured composite fillings that restore damaged teeth to full function without the use of mercury. Virtually invisible — your restored tooth will look completely natural.
