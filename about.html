Who We Are

Your Family's Dental Home

Family Dental Clinic is a modern dental practice located in Gulzar e Quaid, Rawalpindi. Led by Dr. Younas Baloch — a recognised specialist in basal implants and experienced general dentist — we deliver comprehensive dental care in a warm, professional environment.

From routine check-ups and cleanings to complex implant surgeries and orthodontic treatments, every service we provide is rooted in precision, compassion, and a commitment to outstanding outcomes. Our patients consistently highlight our attentive staff, hygienic standards, and transparent, pressure-free approach.

  • Specialist in Basal Implants — advanced permanent tooth replacement
  • PMDC-registered doctors with proven clinical expertise
  • Rigorous sterilisation and clinical hygiene at every step
  • Free initial consultation — no hidden fees, no pressure
  • 6 days a week, 8 AM – 8 PM — convenient for working families
  • Instant WhatsApp booking — book in under 2 minutes
